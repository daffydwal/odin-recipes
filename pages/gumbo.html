Gumbo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 stick of celery, sliced
  • 1 green and 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 400g tin of tomatoes
  • 450ml chicken stock
  • 1 1tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 150g chorizo, chopped into small cubes
  • 150g raw king prawns
  • 1 courgette, cut into small pieces

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the onions, celery and peppers. Cook until started to soften, 5 minutes
  2. Add the chicken, and cook until starting to brown
  3. Add the garlic, flour, and spices, and cook for until the raw flour smell in gone
  4. Pour in the tomatoes, stock, and chorizo
  5. Reduce the heat to a simmer, and cover the pan. Cook for at least 40 minutes
  6. Add in the prawns and courgette, and cook for another 20 minutes
  7. Serve with crusty bread and tabasco sauce on the side.
