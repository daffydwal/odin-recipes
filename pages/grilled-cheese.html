Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2-3 slices cheese, such as gouda
  • 15g Butter

Method

  1. Place a pan on medium-low heat, and melt the butter into the pan. Optionally, add some salt.
  2. Place both slices of bread into the pan and mop the butter up
  3. Place the cheese onto the bread, spreading it out evenly
  4. Cover the pan and sllow the cheese to melt a litte
  5. Take off the lid, and close the sandwich. Continue to cook, flipping occasionally, until the cheese is melted all the way and starting to ooze out of the sides.
  6. Remove the sandwich from the pan, cut into triabgles, and serve.
